OctoAcme practices flexible, iterative project management based on the following principles:
- Customer-first focus
- Iterative delivery in small increments
- Clear ownership (PM & Product Lead)
- Data-driven measurement
- Psychological safety and transparent communication

Our lifecycle spans Initiation, Planning, Execution/Tracking, Release/Deployment, and Retrospective/Improvement, emphasizing stakeholder alignment, visible risk management, and consistent documentation.
